## When to fire

A **material** account that is **dormant** (was spending, silent for a quarter)
or **declining** (spend sliding fast). This play takes precedence over migrate
and grow: there's no point moving a churning account to self-serve or upselling
it — stabilise it first. It's the health guardrail across every segment.

## Offer

Find out what changed and remove the blocker: a supply gap, a pricing issue, a
bad last order, or they've simply drifted to an offline wholesaler. Bring a
concrete reason to come back — new stock in their category, a made-good on the
last issue.

## Message guidance

Personal and low-pressure. Acknowledge the gap ("haven't seen an order since
November"), don't sell hard. Ask an open question and pair it with one concrete
hook (fresh stock they'd want). For the biggest at-risk accounts this is a call,
not a message.
